## Overview

After converting a page, you can send the Markdown content directly into your preferred AI assistant's chat interface — no manual copy-paste needed.

## Supported platforms

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Claude" icon="message">
    [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) — Anthropic's AI assistant. Uses ProseMirror editor injection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ChatGPT" icon="comment">
    [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) — OpenAI's GPT models. Uses textarea injection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gemini" icon="sparkles">
    [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com) — Google's AI. Uses contenteditable injection.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Convert a page">
    Convert any webpage using Web2MD.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send to AI">
    Click the **Send to AI** button in the result panel. Use the dropdown to pick a platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Content is injected">
    Web2MD opens a new tab with the AI platform and automatically injects the Markdown into the chat input.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Fallback behavior

If the injection fails (e.g. the AI platform changes its interface), Web2MD falls back gracefully:

1. Copies the content to your clipboard
2. Opens the AI platform in a new tab
3. Shows a notification: **"Content copied! Press Ctrl+V to paste"**

## Prompt templates

Combine Send to AI with [Prompt Templates](/advanced/prompt-templates) to automatically wrap your content with instructions like "Summarize this article" or "Translate to English".

When a template is selected, the content is wrapped with your prompt before being sent:

```
Please summarize the following article concisely:

[converted Markdown content here]
```

## Auto send

Enable **Auto send to AI** in Settings to automatically send content to your preferred platform after every conversion — no extra click needed.

## Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Preferred AI platform** | Default platform for Send to AI (Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini) |
| **Prompt template** | Template to apply when sending to AI |
| **Auto send to AI** | Automatically send after conversion |

Configure these in **Settings → AI Settings**. [See all settings →](/advanced/settings)
