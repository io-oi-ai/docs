## Overview

Starting in v0.6, every conversion automatically generates a short summary and a set of semantic tags. No configuration needed — Web2MD handles everything behind the scenes so you can scan your history at a glance.

<Note>
AI Summary & Tags is a **Pro-only** feature. Free users see a blurred preview with an upgrade prompt.
</Note>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Convert a page">
    Convert any webpage using the popup, keyboard shortcut, or context menu — the usual flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Summary is generated automatically">
    After conversion completes, Claude Haiku generates a 1–2 sentence summary and 3–5 semantic tags based on the extracted content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View in Preview Card or History">
    The summary and tags appear directly in the Preview Card and in your History list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you get

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Smart Summary" icon="align-left">
    A concise 1–2 sentence summary of the page content. Useful for quickly recalling what an article was about without re-reading it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Semantic Tags" icon="tags">
    3–5 auto-generated tags that capture the key topics. Click any tag to filter your History by that topic.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Where summaries appear

| Location | What you see |
|----------|-------------|
| **Preview Card** | Summary below the stats bar, tags as clickable pills |
| **History list** | Summary snippet under each entry, tags for quick filtering |
| **History search** | Click a tag to find all conversions with that topic |

## Free vs Pro

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pro users">
    Full summary and tags are displayed on every conversion. Click any tag to jump to a filtered History view showing all matching conversions.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Free users">
    Summary and tags are generated but displayed as a **blurred preview**. Click the blurred area to see an upgrade prompt.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Zero configuration

There is nothing to set up. You don't need to provide an API key or enable a toggle — AI Summary & Tags runs automatically on every conversion for Pro users.

<Tip>
Tags are a fast way to build a searchable knowledge base. Over time, your History becomes a topic-indexed library of everything you've converted.
</Tip>
